Scones

Description

These simple scones are very easy to make.
You can add poppy seeds or lemon zest.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for kneading
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg(beaten)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F(200 degrees C). Light grease a baking sheet.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; cut in butter with 2 knives or a pastry blender until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
    Whisk milk and egg in a small bowl; gradually stir into flour mixture until moistened.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly, 5 or 6 turns. Pat or roll dough out onto a 1/2-inch thick round.
    Cut into 8 wedge-shaped pieces and place onto the prepared baking sheets.
  4. Bake scones in the preheated oven until golden brown on top, about 15 minutes.
